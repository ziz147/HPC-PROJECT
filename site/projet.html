NURBS Surfaces for Topology Optimization Problems

Présentation of projetct

NURBS are used to mathematically represent geometric objects. They generalize the representation by B-splines of curves and surfaces by adding a denominator. They are in fact defined with points in homogeneous coordinates. A B-spline resembles a piecewise polynomial representation, while a NURBS is a piecewise rational fraction representation. Particularly used in 3D editing software, these adjustment functions are particularly used in the field of computing, more precisely in image compression and in computer-aided design, in order to generate and represent soft shapes and ergonomic. Ces équations nous permettent de définir des courbes et des surfaces en utilisant des points de contrôle et des poids. Le contrôle précis de ces points de contrôle nous permet de créer des formes complexes avec une grande précision.

Les équations de NURBS sont définies comme suit :

The NURBS surface is defined as:

\begin{equation*} S(u,v) = \sum_{i=0}^{n_u} \sum_{j=0}^{n_v} R_{i,j}(u,v) P_{i,j} \end{equation*} where:

- \( R_{i,j}(u,v) \): Piecewise Rational Basis Functions

-\(P_{i,j}= x_{i,j} , y_{i,j}, z_{i,j}\): Control Points

\begin{equation*} R_{i,j}(u,v) = \frac{N_{i,p}(u)N_{j,q}(v)w_{i,j}}{\sum_{k=0}^{n_u} \sum_{l=0}^{n_v} N_{k,p}(u)N_{l,q}(v)w_{k,l}} \end{equation*} where:\

- \(N_{i,p}(u), N_{j,q}(v)\): Blending Functions

- \(p\) and \(q\): NURBS Degrees along \(u\) and \(v\) directions

\(w_{i,j}\): Weights

\begin{equation*} N_{i,0}(u) = \begin{cases} 1 & \text{if } U_i \leq u < U_{i+1} \\ 0 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \end{equation*} \begin{equation*} N_{i,p}(u) = \frac{u - U_i}{U_{i+p} - U_i} N_{i,p-1}(u) + \frac{U_{i+p+1} - u}{U_{i+p+1} - U_{i+1}} N_{i+1,p-1}(u) \end{equation*}
\begin{equation*} R_{i,j} = 0 \text{ si } (u,v) \text{ est à l'extérieur de } [ U_i, U_{i+p+1}[ \times [ V_j, V_{j+q+1}[ \end{equation*}
